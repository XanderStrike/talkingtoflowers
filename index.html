talkingtoflowers

This was a tumblr blog (perhaps still is) I posted to consistently from 2010 to 2012. It acted as a photo diary during a pivotal time in my early 20s.

These 1,015 images chart a path of growth and change in my photography that parallelled my evolution as a person.

They are presented here today exactly as they would have been, with sparse captions and no comments.
